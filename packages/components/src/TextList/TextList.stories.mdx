## Nested List

You can nest `TextList` by adding another array within the `data` array.

<Canvas>
  <TextList
    data={[
      "Strawberries",
      "Blueberries",
      "Mango",
      "Kiwi",
      [
        "Bananas",
        <TextList
          key="Bananas"
          data={[
            "Burro",
            [
              "Cavendish",
              <TextList
                key="Cavendish"
                data={[
                  "This is the familiar yellow banana sold in United States supermarkets.",
                  [
                    "There are also other sizes of Cavendish",
                    <TextList key="size" data={["Dwarf", "Giant"]} />,
                  ],
                  "Though they are difficult to find",
                ]}
              />,
            ],
          ]}
        />,
      ],
    ]}
  />
</Canvas>
