## Design & usage guidelines

A card is useful for grouping content because of it's distinct visual
boundaries. However, similar to the idea that "making everything stand out means
that nothing stands out", you should be mindful in your application of cards.

A card should be the smallest-possible self-contained section of content. If you
find yourself putting a card inside of another card, the outer card should be
removed, and it may be worth re-assessing the hierarchy of your interface.

### Header with action

Use the header action when there's an action tied directly to the Card's
contents, such as adding more items. It comes with pre-defined styling and
layout to standardize how Card actions look across the product

<Playground>

    header={{
      title: "Banana varieties",

### Header with custom component

Use the provided header title and action properties when possible. If you
require UI elements not supported by the card's header, a custom component can
be passed in.
